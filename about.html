Bio

I was born in Kerman, Iran, in 1992 and lived there until 2016. After completing a Master's degree in Pure Mathematics, I moved to Paris with my husband. In 2019, I returned to mathematics, starting a Master's degree in Applied Mathematics at Sorbonne University. In 2021, I relocated to Vienna, where I continued my studies online alongside taking drawing courses remotely. I subsequently left mathematics behind to focus on painting and drawing. From 2023 to 2025, I have been under the supervision of the Vienna-based artist Hessam Samavatian. Since 2024, I have been based in Gdańsk, Poland.
