Bio

1992-2016

I was born in 1992 in my hometown, Kerman, Iran, where I lived there until 2016.

2016-2021 In 2016, I completed my Master’s in Pure Mathematics and with my husband we moved to Paris. Living there was a distinctive experience; the city’s rich culture and art quietly reignited my interest in painting and drawing. I began to reconnect with art through experiments with oil paints, coloured pencils, and pastels. In 2019, I decided to return to mathematics and began a Master’s in Applied Mathematics at Sorbonne University. 2021-2024 I moved to Vienna in 2021 and has to continue the master's programme online, alongside remote course in drawing. Drawn ever deeper into the world of art, I eventually chose to leave the Master’s behind and devote myself fully to painting—seeking to explore my artistic strengths beyond materials and media. I further refined my skills by taking online courses in art. I have also spent one year working under the supervision of Hessam Samavatian. 2024-2025 I have been based in Gdańsk, Poland. 2025-now I have been based in Warsaw. Despite moving between several places in a relatively short time, I’ve always felt a deep sense of homesickness—of being apart from my motherland, and especially from my family. This feeling has profoundly shaped my artwork. I paint to remember, to hold on to memories. Even I, myself, have become a daily subject—whether in the mirror or reflected in the television screen. I am one of the many figures in my paintings I live alongside.
